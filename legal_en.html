Use of Cookies:

What is a cookie?

Cookies are text files containing small amounts of information that are downloaded to your computer or mobile device when you access our services. We use both session cookies and persistent cookies to make it easier to identify you. A session cookie is used to identify a particular visit to our site. These cookies expire after a short time, or when you close your browser after using our site. A persistent cookie, on the other hand, will remain on your device for a specified period mentioned in the cookie. We use these cookies when we need to identify you over a longer period. For example, when you ask us to keep you logged in.
