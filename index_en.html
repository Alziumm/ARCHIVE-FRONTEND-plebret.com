In 2021, I contributed to the creation of "The Hybrid Project," initiated by the footwear designer Agent33. The project aimed to fund the production chain of his designs. In this context, I designed and developed the entire web application for the project, ensuring a smooth digital experience. KPOVA :

Kpova is an ambitious research project I have been working on for now three and a half years, aiming to revolutionize the analysis of financial markets. This project combines artificial intelligence, financial analysis, and large-scale data management to develop an extremely complex algorithm. The primary goal is to provide real-time and highly accurate market fluctuation analysis, thus enabling informed decision-making for investors and analysts. This project is only in its early stages and has not yet reached a stable version.
